\section*{Data availability}

All datasets used in this study are publicly available. TCGA-BRCA, TCGA PanCancer Atlas cohorts, METABRIC, CPTAC 2020, and SMC 2018 molecular and clinical data were accessed through cBioPortal. GSE96058/SCAN-B data were accessed through the Gene Expression Omnibus and the associated public processed data package. Processed intermediate tables generated for this analysis are stored in the project workspace under \texttt{work/data/}.
